welcome to the schools library!
here you can use a bunch of commands for accessing, deleting or adding data to the base
add using:
	'add student' ==> for adding a student
	'add book' ==> for adding a book
access using:
	'show students' ==> for accessing all students gathered data
	'show student' ==> for accessing a special student's data
	'show books' ==> for accessing all books gathered data
	'show book' ==> for accessing a specific book's data
"remove data:
	'del student' ==> to remove a specific piece of information related to a student
	'del book' ==> to remove a specific piece of information related to a book
